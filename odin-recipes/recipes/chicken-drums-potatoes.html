Chicken Drums with Potatoes

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Pat dry the chicken drumsticks with paper towels.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the diced potatoes with olive oil, minced garlic, dried rosemary, dried thyme, salt, and black pepper. Toss until the potatoes are evenly coated.
  4. Arrange the chicken drumsticks on one side of a baking sheet.
  5. Spread the seasoned potatoes on the other side of the baking sheet.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for about 35-40 minutes or until the chicken is golden brown and the potatoes are tender. Make sure to flip the chicken drumsticks and stir the potatoes halfway through the baking time.
  7. Once cooked, remove from the oven and garnish with lemon slices and chopped fresh parsley.
  8. Serve the chicken drumsticks and potatoes hot. You can pair it with a side salad or your favorite dipping sauce.

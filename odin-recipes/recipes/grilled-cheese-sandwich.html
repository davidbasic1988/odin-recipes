Grilled Sandwich

  1. Preheat your pan or sandwich press.
  2. Choose your favorite bread for the sandwich.
  3. Add your preferred spread on the bread slices (mayonnaise, mustard, etc.).
  4. Layer your favorite ingredients on one slice of bread (cheese, veggies, meats).
  5. Place the second slice of bread on top, creating a sandwich.
  6. Butter the outside of the sandwich for a crispy texture.
  7. Place the sandwich on the preheated pan or sandwich press.
  8. Grill each side until golden brown and the cheese (if added) is melted.
  9. Remove from the pan and let it cool slightly.
  10. Slice and serve your delicious grilled sandwich!

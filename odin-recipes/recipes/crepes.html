Crepes

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1 1/4 whole milk, room temperature
  • 120 gram of all-purpose flour
  • 1 or 2 pinch of kosher salt (optional)
  • Unsalted butter or sunflower oil
  • Fruit, Nutella or honey, for serving (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, blend eggs, milk, flour, sugar, salt, and 3 tbsp melted butter until smooth (1 min).
    Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour or up to 2 days.
  2. Preheat a 9" nonstick skillet over medium heat. Melt 1/2 tsp cold butter in the pan.
    Reduce heat to medium-low. Whisk batter, pour 1/4 cup into the center, tilting to spread. Cook until golden, flip, and cook the other side (45 seconds each).
  3. Use a spatula, fold crêpe into quarters, transfer to plate. Repeat for a total of 8 crêpes.
  4. Serve with berries and powdered sugar, if desired.
